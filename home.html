GREENR
PHILOSOPHY
“You are what you eat” goes the old adage. At GREENR, we live this truth.
We have never been as prosperous as we are today, yet most of us suffer from ill health simply because we eat the wrong food. GREENR is a food initiative to help Indians eat more nutritious, delicious, and sustainable. Yes, it is possible to eat a wholesome vegetarian diet which leaves nothing lacking on the palette. At GREENR, we aspire to create this reality every day.
COMMUNITY
We nurture and cherish the human connection. At GREENR, discover your tribe and become part of a larger movement that’s headed towards a more sustainable world.
SELF
We revere nature in everything we do. We may be urban but our soul is in nature. Always stay connected to your own source, at GREENR.
NATURE
At GREENR, you can be you. No pretense. No comparison. No hierarchy. Just an opportunity to find and love yourself. We love you just the way you are!
We would like to introduce you to GREENR, India's first Plant-pro Cafe. In 2015, Delhi received its first modern vegetarian kitchen, which aims at popularizing plant proteins - a rising phenomenon in food trends.
GREENR is not only a restaurant but a food startup, where we have engineered an indigenous array of plant based proteins by replacing the nutrients and textures we usually get from animal meat with vegetarian sources.
To cater to the increasing demand of roughly 500 million Indian Vegetarians, who are looking for healthy substitutes for meat and for people who like to eat tasty and healthy food without degrading the environment.
