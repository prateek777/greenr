GREENR
PHILOSOPHY
Plantmeats = plant based proteins + superherbs + superfoods
Plant Meat is engineered to maximise the protein content of the plant ingredients used. It uses non dairy products to replicate the meaty, cheesy, creamy flavours and textures of the food that people love to eat at the risk of their health.
