#PLANTPRO
GREENR Cafe is one of India's first modern eateries to be inspired by #Plantpro. Plant pro is a significant development in the food industry which is enabling complex plant-based proteins to become mainstream and give both vegetarians and non-vegetarians nutritious, delicious, and sustainable alternatives to meat.

GREENR
PHILOSOPHY
Plant protein is engineered to maximise the protein content of the plant ingredients used. It uses non dairy products to replicate the meaty, cheesy, creamy flavours and textures of the food that people love to eat at the risk of their health.
Shroomballs
Oh the goodness of Mushrooms and the earthiness of Himalayan Red Kidney Beans! Brown rice, Spinach & Sunflower Seeds; all add even more protein and an element of surprise to these delicious Shroomballs/mince.
BeanPro
Black- eyed Peas, Mushrooms, and Brown Rice dance together in this vegan and dense protein. In combination with our homemade Worcestershire Sauce, you may forget that you are still eating plants!
PeaPro
Bountiful Green Peas are mashed together for this native protein which may remind you of Delhi's kebabs!
Tofu Amaranth
Both Tofu and Amaranth are considered complete proteins in themselves. With this powerhouse mixture, get your protein fix and enjoy the nuttiness and wholesomeness of this 8000 year old grain. Did we mention that this also has Flax Seeds?!
Hemproot
Hemp seeds are considered one of nature's best sources of plant based protein. The seeds contain essential fats and proteins that your body needs to maintain your overall health. Combined with the nutritional powerhouse Beetroot, this Plant Pro abounds in health and taste.
Sweet Potato Falafel
Our take on the classic, the high protein chickpea based Falafel gets a superfood boost with the addition of Sweet Potatoes. This balanced protein is not just a nutritional wonder, but also improves on the timeless Falafel.
